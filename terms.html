Currently, Runwae only requires a shipping number from marketers for material payment. Monetary Payment (Marketers)

Runwae utilizes the Stripe platform to accept payments from Runwae marketers. Marketers are not required to create a stripe account at all. However, payments must be made utilizing card payments and not bank payments. Runwae does not collect the card information at all, but uses a token representative of the card (created by the stripe platform) to charge the card. Runwae does not store this token either.

Minimum payments of $10 are permitted on the Runwae platform for feasibility of operations. Marketers are charged an additional 15% of their payment for processing fees and operating fees.

Monetary Refunds (Marketer)

In the case that the marketer submits payment, the payment will be held by the Runwae platform until the influencer submits proof that they have upheld their end of the agreement. This involves the influencer proving that they have posted their proposed post to the agreed social media platform.

In the case that the influencer does not verify the posted item after five days, the marketer can request a refund of the payment. Since Runwae doesn’t collect card information upon payment, the marketer must enter identical card information upon requesting a refund to receive it.

Note: No refunds will be issued after the influencer accepts the payment because the influencer can only accept the payment after the service has been provided.

Monetary Acceptance (Influencer)

Runwae uses the Stripe platform to allow influencer to accept the payment provided by marketers. Influencers must create a stripe account in order to accept payments on the Runwae platform. Runwae offers influencers an opportunity to do this through the process flow on the Runwae platform. This is done for the benefit of the influencer and Runwae, as Stripe offers a reputable and secure processing solution. Runwae stores only the Stripe user id from the influencer’s account, but Runwae is constructed as to only send payments to influencer’s accounts and to never charge them.

Note: The influencer must send funds from their stripe account to their bank account or configure it to payout automatically.

Note: The influencer has the option to log into their profile and disconnect their Stripe account from their Runwae account at any moment.
