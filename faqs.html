Getting Started

What is Runwae?

At Runwae, our mission is to cultivate meaningful partnerships and opportunities through honest design. We do that through our apparel line and our online platform.

The Runwae platform allows brands to post opportunities to influencers and then guides both parties through a secure agreement and transaction. Our platform is designed to serve a wide range of brands and influencers, but we obsess over micro-influencers and small brands.

Who can sign up?

Whether you're already established or just getting started, Runwae is the place for you. At Runwae, we know that Fast Influencer Marketing, and we allow you to get paid for yours. Even more, you expand your influence and network each time you complete a gig on the Runwae platform.

If you're building your brand on a tight budget, make sure you use the Runwae platform. You can offer gifts to influencers, or name your own price.

Is it free?

It is completely free to sign up on Runwae. Registered users will always have free membership. At this point, we cannot promise that registration will always be free for new users.

Runwae captures 15% of each transaction on the Runwae platform for operating costs. This is captured before the influencer receives payment. Therefore, the influencer receives 85% of the listed price for the gig.

Posts

What is a Runwae Post?

Marketers post opportunities in search of influencers on the Runwae platform. These post can be viewed on the explore page, or the profile page of the marketer. The posts contain a caption describing the opportunity, and may contain a accompanying image. Marketers must specify whether they are offering a gift, or monetary compensation for the opportunity. Marketers must also specify what social media platform they would like influencers to carry out the agreement on.

Users on the Runwae platform can share and comment on posts, and influencers can show interest. Marketers have the option to deactive an item that they've posted so that it is not visible to the public. Once a post is deactivated, a post cannot be reactivated.

Who can post on Runwae?

Marketers are allowed to post opportunities on the Runwae platform. First, you must create an account as a marketer.

Influencers are not allowed to create posts on Runwae.

How do I post on Runwae?

At the top of the explore page, registered marketers will be prompted to create a post on Runwae. Use the caption, platforms, and compensation tabs to navigate.

To complete a post, fill out a caption describing the opportunity you are presenting. On the platform tab, select which social media platforms you are looking to partner with influencers on (you may select multiple). On the compensation tab, select whether you will offer money or gifts as payment. Set the amount of money you wish to offer, or write a description of the gifts you wish to send.

Gigs

What is a gig on Runwae?

A gig on Runwae is an agreement between a marketer and an influencer. There are several steps in completing the gig before it is completed. Gigs are designed to ensure the satisfaction of both parties in the agreement, and to ensure a secure transaction.

The gig begins once a marketer engages an influencer who has shown interest in an opportunity. Upon the engagement, the marketer requests a proposal from the influencer. In the proposal, the influencer should give the marketer a firm idea of what to expect from the agreement and let the marketer know what the social media post will look like.

The marketer may decline the proposal (in which case the influencer can submit another), or submit payment to accept the proposal. If the proposal is accepted then the influencer posts the item on the agreed platform, and then returns to Runwae to verify that it has been posted.

The marketer then checks the appropriate social media platform to see that the item has been posted in agreement with the proposal. Then, the marketer returns to verify the post on Runwae. After the marketer verifies the post, the influencer may accept the payment from the marketer.

How do I start a gig?

A marketer initiates a gig once the engage interests that has been shown on their post. Therefore, a marketer must first post a proposal, then an influencer must show interest in the proposal in order for a gig to be initiated.

How long does a gig last?

A gig can remain open for as long as it takes the two parties to complete it. Either party can decline the gig if no payment has been made. If a payment has been submitted and either party is unsatisfied, email us at team@runwae.com, or report the dispute directly on Runwae.

Interest

What is interest on Runwae?

An influencer expresses that they are interested in an opportunity posted by a marketer.

How do I show interest?

On a Runwae post, social media icons are listed which represent the social media platforms that the marketer is looking to promote on. Influencers can show interest in a partnership for that platform by clicking the appropriate social media icon.

Once you have shown interest in a platform, the color will change to reflect that the interest has been shown.

Can I remove interest?

Once you show interest in a post on Runwae, you cannot remove the interest shown on the post. Your interest does not hold you to any responsibility.

Followers

What are followers on Runwae?

Users of the same role may follow each other on the Runwae platform. An influencer cannot follow a marketer, and vice versa. The people who follow you are displayed on your profile, as well as the people that you follow.

Your Runwae activity feed is populated with public actions taken by people that you follow.

Who should I follow?

You should follow users that you are interested in learning more about, users who have similar partnerships, or users who share similar interests. You may unfollow users whenever you choose.

Who can follow me?

Anyone user of the same role may follow you on the Runwae platform. Currently, Runwae does not have the capability to allow users to block other users.

Favorites

What are favorites on Runwae?

Users of opposite roles may follow each other on the Runwae platform. An influencer cannot favorite a influencer, and a marketer cannot favorite a marketer. The people who favorite you are displayed on your profile, as well as the people that you favorite.

Favorites only reflect your interests to those who visit your profile and allow you to keep track of your interests.

Who should I favorite?

Favorite pages that you find interesting or satisfactory. You may unfavorite pages whenever you choose.

Who can favorite me?

Any user of the opposite role may favorite your page on Runwae.

Messaging

What is Runwae messaging?

Runwae messagin is a conversation between a marketer and an influencer. They allow both parties to communicate intentions and desires in an agreement, and to maintain communication after the transaction.

Who can I message?

You can only message a user once a gig is initiated between you. Therefore, users of the same role cannot message each other on Runwae. This is done so that we distinguish communication between marketers and influencers on Runwae in order to preserve the importance and focus of such.

How do I message?

Once you enter a gig, click the manage tab, and you will find the button prompting you to message your partner.

Shares

What are shares on Runwae?

Users share a post to communicate their interest in the post to their followers. Currently, shares only appear on the activity feed, and not on the profile of the user who shared the item.

How do I share?

Click the paper plane icon next to the comment icon when viewing a post. The icon will change colors to reflect tha you have shared the item. You may not unshare an item once you have shared it, but this action only appears on the activity feed of your followers, and not your profile.

Who can share my content?

Anyone may share content on the Runwae platform, except the user that has posted it.

Payment

How do I get paid?

Influencers receive payment during the process of completing a gig with a marketer. This step occurs after the marketer has verified that the influencer posted the proposal to the specified social media platform.

Marketers never recieve payment on Runwae unless it is determined that the marketer will receive a refund in a dispute.

Is my payment secure?

Runwae uses Stripe for all payments on the Runwae platform. Runwae only receives a token representing your payment information and never stores this token, or any payment information belonging to the marketer or the influencer.

When will I be charged?

Influencers are never charged on the Runwae platform. The marketer is during the gig process when they accept the proposal from the influencer. Though the marketer is charged immediately, this payment is not sent to the influencer until it is verified that they have followed thorugh on the agreement.
