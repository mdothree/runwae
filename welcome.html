How It Works

The Runwae Agreement Process

Proposal

The Influencer submits a proposal to the marketer for the marketing opportunity.

Payment

The marketer is pleased with the influencer's proposal and submits payment.

Post

The influencer posts the proposal to social media.

Verification

The marketer verifies that the influencer posted the agreed content.

Payout

The influencer receives their payment.
